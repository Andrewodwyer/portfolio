UX/UI Designer & Developer

My Journey

I'm Andrew, a UX/UI designer and full-stack developer with a background deeply rooted in visual arts. My journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Fine Art & Design from Crawford College of Art and Design, where I developed a strong foundation in visual composition, colour theory, and creative problem-solving through painting and photography.

After college, I spent five years refining my skills as a photographer before establishing my own photography business in 2011. To further develop my technical abilities, I studied Photography and Digital Imaging at the National College of Art and Design in Dublin, which enhanced my understanding of visual communication and digital media.

In 2024, I decided to evolve my career towards design. My experience managing a business built on visual design and aesthetics, combined with years of client collaboration, naturally led me to UX/UI design. This transition was supported by completing a Diploma in Full Stack Development from Code Institute, where I designed and developed several projects from concept to fully functioning websites and e-commerce platforms.

Throughout my projects, I've focused on creating applications that are both creative and intuitive. I follow the Design Thinking approach, placing users at the centre of the design process and iterating through wireframes to create experiences that are engaging and natural to navigate.

My Approach to Design

Visual Aesthetics

My background in fine arts and photography has given me a strong sense of composition, colour harmony, and visual storytelling. I apply these principles to create interfaces that are not only functional but visually engaging.

User-Centered Design

I believe in designing for people first. My process involves understanding user needs, creating personas, and developing solutions that address real problems. I iterate based on feedback to ensure the final product is intuitive and enjoyable to use.

Attention to Detail

Photography taught me the importance of precision and detail. In UX/UI design, I apply this same meticulous approach to ensure consistency across interfaces, from typography and spacing to interaction patterns and micro-animations.

Technical Understanding

My full-stack development skills allow me to design with implementation in mind. I understand technical constraints and possibilities, which helps me create designs that are not only beautiful but feasible to build.
