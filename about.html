About Me

I'm Andrew, a UX/UI designer and full-stack developer passionate about creating intuitive and visually appealing digital experiences. My background in fine arts and photography has cultivated my eye for detail and composition, which I now apply to the digital realm.

With expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, I combine design principles with technical skills to develop comprehensive solutions. I'm driven by the challenge of transforming complex problems into simple, elegant solutions that users find both functional and enjoyable.

My approach to UX/UI design is rooted in understanding user needs and behaviours. I believe in creating interfaces that not only look great but also provide seamless interactions. This user-centric philosophy, combined with my technical skills, allows me to create designs that are both aesthetically pleasing and technically feasible.

In my previous role as a Creative Director in photography, I honed my skills in visual storytelling and brand identity creation. These experiences have proven invaluable in my transition to digital design, enabling me to create cohesive and impactful user experiences across various digital platforms.

I'm committed to staying current with the latest design trends and technological advancements. When I'm not designing or coding, you'll find me exploring new photography techniques or skiving off to art workshops.
