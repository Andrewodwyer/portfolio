My Journey So Far

I started my creative journey with a Bachelor's in Fine Art & Design from Crawford College of Art and Design. This foundation in visual arts honed my eye for composition and storytelling – skills that I now apply to crafting user-centered designs.

For over a decade, I've been running my own photography and design business. This experience taught me the value of understanding client needs and translating them into visual solutions – a skill that translates perfectly to UX/UI design.

Eager to expand my toolkit, I recently completed a Diploma in Full Stack Software Development from Code Institute. Now, I combine my artistic background with technical skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python to create seamless digital experiences from concept to implementation.

My approach to UX/UI design is rooted in understanding user needs and behaviors. I believe in creating interfaces that not only look great but also provide seamless interactions.

My portfolio showcases a range of projects, from e-commerce platforms to event management apps, each demonstrating my commitment to user-focused design and clean, efficient code.

I'm always excited to take on new challenges and collaborate on projects that make a real difference in people's digital lives. Let's create something amazing together!
